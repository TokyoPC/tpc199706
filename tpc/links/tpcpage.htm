Introduction

The links at this TPC site contain information about the technical aspects of the Internet and World Wide Web for enhancing the time you spend in using this exciting new media. It also includes links to technical information about operating computers, especially in Japan.

You can find links here that highlight the people and organizations behind the creation and maintenance of the Net and Web and their protocols and standards, technical meta-references for webmasters and system administrators, security, fair use and copyright policy, and statistics that reveal continuing exponential growth in this media.

Sites where you can find up-to-date news are listed under e-zines and online journals, and upcoming events can be found under conferences and expositions.

If you are looking for an overview of the WWW and Internet, look at links listed under Introduction to Cyberspace. For quite a few accounts of how humanity has come to this point in technological history, see links under History of Cyberspace. And if you want some views on where this technology may be taking us, look at links under Cyberculture.

So you can derive maximum benefit from the Web's myriad multimedia capabilities, links to tools and plugins are also included. Computing and Internet use in Japan present unique challenges to users, so TPC has also included links to Japanese browsers, software and search engines.

If you are interested in improving your computing skills via education and training, you can select from a growing number of providers that deliver courseware over the Net and Web. TPC has included a set of links for browsing these opportunities.

Since TPC is best known as a computer club, it has listed links to sites that offer a variety information about computing. Microprocessor and computational history has been especially popular with web-site builders and many of their links are included here. For those of you who are bold enough to build your own machines, you will find links to information about computer software and hardware. If you have questions, you can find help at the links listed under Web and computer help, computer dictionaries and glossaries, computer associations and places for used computer equipment.

Finally, TPC has included links for users interested in information technology in Japan and networking and telecommunications information.
