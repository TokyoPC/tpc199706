TPC Membership & Meetings

This information is current as of April 1997

TPC Membership

Full membership of the Tokyo PC Users Group, including a subscription to Algorithmica Japonica, full access to the TPC BBS, free meeting attendance, and other benefits, is 10,000 Yen per year plus a one-time 3,000 Yen initiation fee. (Corporate, family, and student memberships are also available.) A BBS-only membership is 4,000 Yen for one year. A one-year mailed subscription to Algorithmica Japonica is available for 6,000 Yen to non-members. Call the TPC Hotline for information, or contact any of the TPC officers.

TPC Monthly Meetings

Meetings are held on the first Thursday of the month in the basement of the The Tokyo Union Church on Omotesando Dori. Doors open at 6:30 and the meetings begin at 7 p.m. Attendance fee for non-members is 1,000 Yen.
