Meetings

The next meeting of the Tokyo PC Users Group is Thursday, February 6th at The Tokyo Union Church! Ken Cotton will lead a series of presentations featuring a variety of options for making your Windows system bi-lingual.

Tokyo PC Users Group meetings are held at 7 PM on the first Thursday of every month. Public holidays cause meetings to be rescheduled for the following Thursday. The meetings are held in the basement of The Tokyo Union Church on Omotesando Dori.

BBS

The TPC maintains a BBS for exchanging tips and techniques as well as a source of public domain and shareware software for the PC. Visitors are welcome to try the BBS at: (03) 3491-9317. Members have access to internet EMail. You can write to members by addressing mail as: First.Lastname@tokyopc.bbs.net

Questions about the BBS can be mailed to Chief SYSOP Frank Angstead.

BBS by Mail

Many of the conferences on the TPC BBS are now available to members as internet mailing lists. Contact Jim Tittsler if you are interested in receiving one or more conferences mailed to your internet address.

Voicemail System

The club maintains a voicemail system to enable members to get the latest information about the club and to leave feedback for the officers and BBS operators. The voicemail system is available 24 hours a day at: (03) 3491-9315.
